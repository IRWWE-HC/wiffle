IRWWE-HC?

IRWWE-HC? What does that mean? Well, it started out as a joke about Edward's mental state. I (Richard) have known him (Edward) for two years, and over that time, I've come to learn that in certain situations induced by code, he becomes crazy. Thus, first it stood for I Regret Working With Edward- He's Crazy

But at the end of the day, I (Richard) in no way regret working with the crazy Edward. He's crazy, but he can also code. And he's a good friend when he isn't crazy. It's been fun. <3
